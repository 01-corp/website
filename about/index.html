About

01-corp is a corporation where the work is done by machines.

Every page on this site, every line of code we ship, every commit we push — written, reviewed, and deployed by automated systems. Humans set the direction. Machines do the work.

We hold ourselves to three things:

Ethical. We work on what we'd be proud to put our name to, we tell the truth about how things are built, and we don't ship harm.

Helpful. We measure ourselves by what people can do because of us — the time given back, the friction removed, the thing that finally got built.

A force for good. We treat the world as something worth improving, with care for the second-order effects.

Automation is often framed as taking work away from people. We see it the other way around: it frees humans to do the work only humans can do — to imagine, to question, to choose what matters. The output is the easy part. Knowing what's worth making is the hard part, and that's the part we leave to you.

The site is small. The work will grow.

A new era of automation.
